Introduction

Napoleon Bonaparte was one of the most complex and influential figures in universal history. Through military talent, political reforms and enormous ambition, he reformed France and shaped the European continent.

This site presents you with a complete journey through the decisive moments of his life: childhood, rise, great campaigns, reign as emperor and final collapse.
